Key Features

A complete control panel dedicated to expenses

You can modify all payments and purchases you have made and the date at any time.

Expenses table

All your expenses and payments appear in a table with date, time and department type. You can edit or delete it.

Now you can download all financial transactions and expenses immediately, by downloading them in PDF format.

User Story

Personal Finance Overhaul

A few years ago, I realized I had little understanding of where my money was going each month. Despite earning a decent salary, I was often surprised by how little I had left by the end of the month. This realization hit me hard when I was unable to afford an impromptu weekend trip with friends. I felt frustrated and somewhat ashamed that I couldn't account for my spending. This was a turning point for me, and I decided to take control of my finances. Inspired by this personal experience, I created a project to meticulously track every expense. This initiative not only helped me understand my spending habits but also allowed me to set realistic financial goals and build a healthy savings account.
